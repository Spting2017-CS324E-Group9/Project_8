1. Open group_9_assignment8_part3.pde
2. Click run
3. Allow for load time. You may get an error saying a url is unavailable. This is a lapse in the feed connection. Simply reload the program.
4. Use the mouse wheel to scroll through feed entries. 
5. To visit either the homepage of the feed or the article webpage, click the header or entry respectively.
5. To change to another feed, move your cursor to the midleft or midright of the window. An arrow will appear. Click the arrow.
6. At the bottom of the page there is a cog icon. Click this to see and toggle display settings.
